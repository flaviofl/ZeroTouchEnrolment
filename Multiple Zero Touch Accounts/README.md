# Multiple Zero Touch Accounts

A customer organization can have multiple Zero Touch accounts, typically in these scenarios:

| Scenario                    | How It Works                                                                                                                                  |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Separate legal entities** | Each subsidiary/division has its own Google Workspace domain and its own Zero Touch customer account                                          |
| **Different regions**       | Global companies often maintain separate accounts per region for compliance or billing reasons                                                |
| **Different resellers**     | Devices purchased through different resellers may end up in different customer accounts (though a single account can have multiple resellers) |
| **M\&A activity**           | Acquired companies may retain separate accounts during transition                                                                             |
