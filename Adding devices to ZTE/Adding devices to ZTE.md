# Adding devices to ZTE

## How to get new purchased devices added to ZTE
Requires the ZTE configured
Inform the reseller during purchase to add devices to the customer zero touch account

## How to get existent devices devices added to ZTE
1. Contact your original reseller/vendor — Provide them with a list of IMEIs or serial numbers
2. The Reseller will upload the devices via the ZTE reseller portal (https://enterprise.google.com/android/zero-touch/resellers/) or Reseller integrated application
3. Devices appear in your customer portal — Then you can assign it via configuration on the MDM platform
